AEGIS League

Welcome to AEGIS Organized Play!

Whenever you play a game in real life or in Tabletop Simulator, you can simply report your opponent and the scores, and they will be recorded on the AEGIS League Leaderboard!

For more information, join the AEGIS Discord!

AEGIS League Season 3 (September - December 2020)

Season 3 ends on January 1st. Anyone who participates will get a special promo card mailed to them at the end of the season. In addition, the top league players will be invited to participate in a tournament at the end of the season with cash (gift card) prizes.

*** This season, players will have the opportunity to play with new AEGIS cards from the game's upcoming expansion! Playing with those and reporting feedback will get you listed in the next rulebook.
