AEGIS League

Welcome to AEGIS Organized Play!

Whenever you play a game in real life or in Tabletop Simulator, you can simply report your opponent and the scores, and they will be recorded on the AEGIS League Leaderboard!

For more information, join the AEGIS Discord!

AEGIS League Season 3 (September - December 2020)

Season 3 ends on January 23rd. Anyone who participates will get a special promo card mailed to them at the end of the season. In addition, the top league players will be invited to participate in a tournament at the end of the season with cash (gift card) prizes.

*** This season, players will have the opportunity to play with new AEGIS cards from the game's upcoming expansion! Playing with those and reporting feedback will get you listed in the next rulebook.

AEGIS Winter Tournament

Compete in the AEGIS Winter Tournament for the chance to win cash and the opportunity to make your own promo card! The tournament is free to enter, and all skill levels are welcome! Sign up here!

Tournament Rules

  • Bring a team of five robots, a sideboard of 2 robots (which can be swapped out between games), and a combine pool of up to 10 combine robots!
  • All robots except for EXCEL-GAN Super Mode and ELL-GEL-ION Awakening are legal to use! This includes AEGIS 2 robots and promos!
  • The tournament uses a double-elimination bracket. Matches will be best 2-of-3.
  • Scrap combining will not be used.

There are a few rule changes from AEGIS 1 that will be accounted for this tournament:

  • Turn 1 Armistice. A player cannot be attacked until that player has taken a turn. This means that the first player cannot attack the second player, but the second player can attack the first!
  • Board Variations! Both the standard 2-Player AEGIS board and the AEGIS2 "Skinny Board" are going to be used. The first round of each best 2-of-3 match will have a randomly chosen board, and after round 1, the loser will choose the board. Players can sideboard before the first game after seeing which board they will be playing on!
